Who and What is this service for?

⠀⠀⠀⠀This service is for people who want to have ease of mind when browsing and doing transactions online. If you are worried about people learning your personal info or you have a significant presence online; you have come to the right place. I use my experience and expertise to look into you, then send a report on what I have found. If I find things that you consider to be information you want private, then I will give you steps with how you can cover it up/ remove it. The intent of this service is not to assist in malicious activity, rather to promote online security. I will only investigate the client that is paying me. I will not look into other entities - any such attempt will result in termination of service immediately and I will contact the user/ entity that was attempted to be investigated and make them aware. In such a case the ex-client's information will not be disclosed to the individual. Just that someone is trying to dig up information on them.

Why Should i care?

⠀⠀⠀⠀Whether you realize it or not, you give out a lot of very personal information online. If someone targets you and your information, what would they be able to find out about you? If you get doxxed or if someone finds out the wrong information they can make both your online and real life Hell. Someone could spam delivery under your name to you address; say that you are involved in something illegal so that the police show up at your door; steal your identity and accounts. Would you be okay knowing that your photos, emails, or chats could - in the hands of someone who can use that information - against you?
